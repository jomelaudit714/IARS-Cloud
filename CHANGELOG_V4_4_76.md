# Changelog — V4.4.76

## Dashboard Weekly Itinerary behavior

- Administrators retain the Pending Approval counter and module shortcut.
- The administrator's own approved current-week itinerary image now appears immediately below the approval summary.
- Older administrator submissions are matched by auditor name and submitted username, even when their historical owner key was stored as `admin`.
- Another administrator's itinerary is not treated as the signed-in administrator's personal itinerary.
- Ordinary auditors see their approved current-week JPG/PNG directly on the Dashboard.
- Pending ordinary-auditor submissions show `Status: Waiting for Approval` instead of an image.
- Returned submissions show `Returned for Revision` and the administrator remarks.

## Dashboard and sidebar refinements

- Dashboard greeting now ends with an exclamation mark.
- Welcome heading font is larger and heavier for clearer visibility.
- EDL sidebar logo receives full-width centering and visual offset correction for the PNG's asymmetric transparent padding.
- Header and Settings version labels are both updated to 4.4.76.

## Unchanged

- Generate Extraction and Operations Audit rules
- PDF Tagging
- Shared PDF Archive and duplicate detection
- Policies & Memoranda company folders
- Weekly Itinerary upload, approval, return, revision, and storage processes
- Authentication, user management, and Master Data
