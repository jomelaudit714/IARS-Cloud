# Changelog — V4.4.77

## Dashboard lower-row alignment

- The Weekly Itinerary panel now uses exactly three of the five Dashboard card-width units.
- Its right edge aligns with the right edge of the **Archived PDFs** metric card above it.
- The Recent Archive Activity panel retains its existing two-card width and position.
- The gap between both panels is reduced from large to medium without resizing the archive panel.
- Approved JPG/PNG itinerary images are constrained to the Weekly Itinerary panel width.
- The image uses proportional scaling and cannot overflow into Recent Archive Activity.
- The Welcome heading remains `Welcome back, <name>!`; no extra characters are appended.

## Unchanged

- Weekly Itinerary submission and approval workflow
- role-based personal itinerary display
- Recent Archive Activity content and panel behavior
- metric-card sizes
- sidebar and logo
- Generate Extraction, PDF Tagging, archive, libraries, and authentication
